Lady Writing a Letter with her Maid is a painting by the Dutch artist Johannes Vermeer,completed in 1670–1671 and held in the National Gallery of Ireland.The work shows a middle-class woman attended by a housemaid who is presumably acting as messenger and go-between for the lady and her lover. The work is seen as a bridge between the quiet restraint and self-containment of Vermeer's work of the 1660s and his relatively cooler work of the 1670s
